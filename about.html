🌅 About MoodSpace

What this app is and how it works

← Back

MoodSpace is a simple, lightweight mood-journaling app. Check in with how you're feeling, jot down what's on your mind, and get a short motivational quote picked just for your mood — all in your browser, with nothing to install and nothing to sign up for.

Your entries are saved locally on your device, so your journal stays private to you. The "World Mood" tab is a simulated community feed, illustrating what a real-time shared mood board could look like.

Features
  • 📝Daily mood check-ins across five levels — Awful, Sad, Okay, Good, and Great — with an optional note.
  • ✨A personalized motivational quote after every check-in, matched to your mood.
  • 📈A history view with a mood chart and a list of your past entries.
  • 🌍A "World Mood" feed showing aggregate stats and recent check-ins.
  • 🔒No accounts, no servers — your data lives only in your own browser.

MoodSpace is a personal journaling tool and is not a substitute for professional mental health support. If you're struggling, please reach out to a mental health professional or a crisis line in your area.
